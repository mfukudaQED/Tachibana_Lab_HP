QEDynamics (4 component version)

 
 

QEDynamics(4 component version) is a Fortran 90 program which simulates time evolution of atomic or molecular systems based on the Rigged QED theory. This website introduces the one based on the method which uses 4-component electronic field.

 

“Electron-positron oscillations” in time variation of charge density of hydrogen atom

(K. Ichikawa, M. Fukuda and A. Tachibana, Int. J. Quant. Chem. 113, 190 (2013))
